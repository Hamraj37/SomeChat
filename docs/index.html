About the App

Same Chat is a modern Android messaging application designed for fast and reliable communication. Built with Material 3 and Firebase, it provides a familiar yet enhanced experience with dynamic theme support.

Real-time Messaging

Chat instantly with friends and family using Firebase Realtime Database. Features include last message previews and delivery status.

Voice & Video Calls

High-quality audio and video calls powered by WebRTC. Switch cameras, mute, and use speakerphone seamlessly.

Online Presence

Know when your friends are online with WhatsApp-style real-time status indicators and "Last Seen" timestamps.

Material You

Full support for Monet dynamic colors. The app's interface automatically adapts to your wallpaper and system theme.

Offline Access

Your chat list is saved locally using Room database, so you can access your conversations even without an internet connection.

Secure Authentication

Sign in quickly and securely with your Email or Google account.
